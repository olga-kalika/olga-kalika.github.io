about me

I am graghic designer from Ukraine. I’m in love with typography and everything related to it. Typography is melody of my soul. My «typographyc» style exists since 2015 which I embody in my works and crazy ideas to this day.

bodyart

I always adore fonts and nudes. I was insanely inspired to mix these things together, feel the instuments on sensitive skin and a vibrant breathing. I did my first bodyart work for Franko Laboratorium exhibition in 2017.

my principles

All of my works based on expression. As for me, art is a way of exploring and expressing oneself. Honestly, I can`t do just something beautiful. Sometimes, my works can look some creppy or dirty but I know that it translate my actually mental state and that`s why it worth to be.
